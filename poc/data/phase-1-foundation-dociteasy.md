## 7. Domain Expert Knowledge

### The Document Collection Problem
The core pain: B2B service firms (legal, finance, HR, real estate) spend enormous time chasing clients for documents. The typical workflow:
1. Send client a list of required documents via email
2. Client sends some, misses others, sends wrong formats
3. Multiple follow-up emails/calls
4. Manual checking of received documents
5. Filing and organizing received documents
6. Compliance verification (is the document valid, current, correct?)

This process is:
- **Time-consuming**: 5-15 hours per client onboarding for complex cases
- **Error-prone**: Missing documents cause delays and compliance risk
- **Frustrating**: Both for the professional and the client
- **Costly**: Staff time spent on admin instead of billable work

### Why AI Matters Here
1. **Document Classification**: Automatically identify document types (passport, bank statement, utility bill, etc.)
2. **Completeness Checking**: Flag missing documents automatically
3. **Data Extraction**: Pull key data from submitted documents
4. **Predictive Alerts**: Predict which clients are likely to delay and proactively nudge them
5. **Compliance Validation**: Check documents meet regulatory requirements

### Industry-Specific Nuances
- **Legal (UK/Ireland)**: SRA compliance, Anti-Money Laundering checks, Conveyancing document requirements
- **Mortgage Brokers**: FCA-regulated, extensive documentation requirements (income proof, ID, property details)
- **Accountants**: Tax filing deadlines create seasonal urgency for document collection
- **HR/Recruitment**: Right-to-work checks, reference collection, onboarding documentation

### Key Success Factors for This Market
1. **Security & Compliance**: GDPR, SOC 2, encryption — non-negotiable for legal/finance
2. **Client Experience**: The client-facing portal must be dead simple (clients are non-technical)
3. **Integrations**: Must connect to existing tools (Clio, Xero, practice management systems)
4. **White-labeling**: Firms want their brand on the client-facing portal
5. **Audit Trail**: Every document interaction must be logged for compliance
