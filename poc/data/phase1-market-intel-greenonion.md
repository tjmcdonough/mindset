# Phase 1.3: Market Intel — GreenOnion

## Market Size

### Amazon Seller Ecosystem
- **~9.7 million** Amazon sellers worldwide, **~1.9-2.5 million active**
- Third-party sellers represent **60%+** of all Amazon sales
- Amazon marketplace GMV: **$2.5 trillion+ cumulative** through sellers

### TAM/SAM/SOM Estimates [ALL INFERRED]
- **TAM:** All ecommerce sellers needing product images globally — ~10M+ sellers across Amazon, Etsy, Shopify, eBay, Walmart
- **SAM:** Amazon/Etsy sellers who need listing image help and would pay for a tool — ~500K-1M (those with 10+ SKUs, doing $10K+/yr)
- **SOM (Year 1 realistic):** 500-2,000 paying customers at $49-149/mo = $300K-$3.6M ARR

### Adjacent Markets
- Amazon seller tools market (Helium 10, Jungle Scout, etc.) — multi-billion dollar ecosystem
- AI product photography — emerging, $500M+ and growing rapidly
- Freelance design services for Amazon (Fiverr, agencies) — $1B+ displacement opportunity

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Focus | Pricing | Key Differentiator |
|-----------|-------|---------|-------------------|
| **Ecomtent.ai** | A+ Content + listings, Amazon/Walmart/eBay | $510/mo for 25 SKUs | Most direct competitor. GEO optimization for RUFUS/COSMO. Enterprise-focused. |

| **Assembo.ai** | AI product photos/videos for social | $9-49/mo | Social-first, not Amazon-specific. |
| **Claid.ai** | AI image enhancement API | API pricing | B2B API, not self-serve for sellers. |
| **Galaxy.ai** | AI Amazon listing image generator | Unknown | New entrant. |

### Indirect Competitors
- **Canva** — Templates for Amazon, increasingly AI-powered. $12.99/mo. THREAT: Could add one-click Amazon listing generation.
- **Adobe Firefly** — AI image generation integrated into Photoshop. If they add ecommerce templates, strong threat.
- **Midjourney** — Used by sellers already for lifestyle images (Reddit confirms). Requires prompting skill.
- **Freelancers (Fiverr/Upwork)** — $50-500 per listing design. The "incumbent" being displaced.
- **Amazon's own tools** — Amazon already adding AI listing tools. Could build this natively.

### Competitive Analysis Summary

**GreenOnion's unique position:** Zero-prompt, full 9-image set generation from one photo. Most competitors require prompting, do single images, or are broader tools.

**Biggest threats (ranked):**
1. **Ecomtent** — Most similar, better funded, enterprise positioning, $510/mo validates higher pricing
2. **Pic Copilot** — Free, Alibaba-backed, data moat
3. **Amazon native tools** — Platform risk if Amazon builds this in
4. **Canva/Adobe** — If they add ecommerce-specific workflows
5. **Photoroom** — Already has seller traction, well-funded ($43M Series B)

## Key Market Dynamics
- Amazon sellers are **price-sensitive but ROI-driven** — they'll pay if they see conversion lift
- **A+ Content adoption is growing** — Amazon pushing brands to use it, making it available to more sellers
- **AI image quality improving rapidly** — what was "wow" 6 months ago is table stakes now
- **Consolidation happening** — Amazon seller tools converging (Helium 10 bought many tools)
- **Amazon itself is adding AI tools** — direct platform risk
